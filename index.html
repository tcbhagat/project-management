*GCOE Amravati CREATIVE MINDS are all set to produce inventive solutions on Anrdoid apps. This page helps collaborate their activities and keeps track of students contribution. The participation is all voluntary and encourages all CREATIVE MINDS. *

Students are advised to study the trends and exert to make out-of-the box suggestions. The participants get opportunity to develop their skills in Android App Development for engineering and professional solutions. The training from time to time will be arranged for the developers.

The following are some of the projects in progress

  • Library books availability notification: To broadcast notification to students about library book availability.
  • gOLX for gcoea campus : To enable GCOEA students make sale-purchase of old item.
  • Project voter: Mobile voting to choose the worth of a project on weekly basis.
  • Project scheduler: Create a project schedule in the form of a bar chart.
  • College Assignments: To help class room assignment, evaluation and submission by mobile.
